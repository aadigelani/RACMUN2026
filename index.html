About the Conference

A Forum for the Uncommon Voice

The Rotaract Club of St. Xavier's College, Ranchi(RID=3250) proudly presents its Rotaract MUN under Vocational Service Avenue, a dynamic platform designed to shape the leaders of tomorrow.

More than just a conference, Rotaract MUN is an experience where young minds step into the world of diplomacy, engage in thought provoking debates, and sharpen their critical thinking on global issues. It is a space that nurtures confidence, communication, and collaboration.

Rooted in the true essense of Rotaract, this initiative beautifully blends leadership, service, and youth engagement, creating an environment where ideas turn into impact. Here, voices matter, perspectives evolve, and solutions are born, truly making it a place where diplomacy meets youth leadership. We invite you to step into chambers where history is debated — and perhaps, remade.

Conference Itinerary

A meticulously curated day of diplomacy.

09:00 AM

Delegate Registration

Delegates arrive, collect credentials. An opportunity for early networking.

10:00 AM

Inaugural Ceremony

Formal opening of RACMUN 2026. Address by the Secretary General, Chief Guest, and Faculty Advisors.

10:30 AM

Committee Session I

Opening of formal debate. Roll call, setting of the agenda, general speakers list, and initial positioning speeches.

12:30 PM

Luncheon Recess

A curated lunch break. Delegates may continue informal lobbying and coalition-building.

01:30 PM - 4:00 PM

Moderated and unmoderated caucuses. Working papers presented. Bloc negotiations intensify.
